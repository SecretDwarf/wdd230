Target Audience:

Who: Any person wanting to get a quick look at what's going on in their hometown and promote or know more about business around them. Ages 10-100, who have access to an Internet connected device.

People:

Occupation: Homemaker

Motivation to use the site: Wants benefits from local businesses.

Occupation: Small business owner.

Motivation to use the site: Wants for promote his business and network with other businesses.

Scenarios:

  1. What are some upcoming events in my area?
  2. Being new to the area, I want to know more about Rigby, Idaho
  3. How can find other businesses to network with?
  4. I want to learn the inner-workings of the Rigby community,
  5. How can I be more involved in addressing the challenges and issues in our community?
